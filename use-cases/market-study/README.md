# Market Study

Generate institutional-grade market study reports with quarterly KPI matrices, supply-demand analysis, and investor metrics. Covers price performance, supply-demand dynamics, seller behavior, investor activity, and portfolio operator trends.

![Market Study Demo](../../assets/gifs/market-study_small.gif)

## What You Get

A comprehensive PDF report with:

- Institutional KPI matrices (All Properties, Single Family, Luxury) with QoQ and YoY comparisons
- Supply-demand divergence charts
- Seller stress bubble charts by submarket
- Investor acquisition/disposition analysis
- Market outlook and classification

## Choose Your Path

### Basic (Copy & Paste)

Copy the prompt from [`basic/PROMPT.md`](basic/PROMPT.md) into Claude Code. Targets the Hamptons by default. Change the market name for any area.

### Advanced (Skill)

Run `/market-study [market name]` for a fully automated report with:

- Listing session reconstruction for historical KPI matrices
- Automated benchmark comparison (MSA, state, national)
- Anti-AI writing rules for professional narrative voice
- Institutional-grade visualization standards

See the [skill definition](../../.claude/skills/market-study/SKILL.md) for full details.

## Analytical Framework

Uses the Parcl Labs Housing Intelligence Methodology covering:

- Real-time market conditions (YoY, YTD, peak-to-current, since COVID)
- Supply-demand gap (3-month moving average method)
- Market correction classifications (bear market, correction territory, approaching correction)
- Seller behavior benchmarks (price cut rate vs 41% national average)
- Investor metrics (purchase-to-sale ratio, ownership share, gross yield)
